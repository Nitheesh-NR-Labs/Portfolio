Currently Working On

AETHRA Flight Controller

A fully integrated, next-generation UAV flight controller designed for performance, redundancy, and clean builds.

AETHRA redefines the conventional drone stack by consolidating flight control processing, power distribution and regulation, redundant inertial and environmental sensing, onboard telemetry, and peripheral expansion into a single, integrated flight controller architecture.

The system combines the MCU stack, PDB, dual IMUs, dual barometers, magnetometer, telemetry interfaces, and onboard diagnostics within a compact form factor, reducing interconnect complexity, improving EMI performance, and enabling deterministic, low-latency control behavior.

Core

Dual-MCU Architecture

High-performance main processor with a dedicated I/O co-processor for sensor handling and failsafe logic.

Integration

All-in-One Stack

Integrated PDB, sensors, telemetry, and display — eliminating messy wiring and compatibility issues.

Reliability

Redundant Sensors

Dual IMUs, dual barometers, and multiple communication paths for fault tolerance and safety.

Design

Modular ESC System

Side-mounted, clip-in ESC modules that remove the need for soldering and bulky wiring harnesses.

UX

On-Board Display

Integrated display for battery status, GPS lock, flight mode, and telemetry at a glance.

Vision

Indian-Made, Global-Grade

Designed to compete with premium international flight controllers while being manufactured efficiently in India.
