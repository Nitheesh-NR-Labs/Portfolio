Currently Working On

AETHRA Flight Controller

A fully integrated, next-generation UAV flight controller designed for performance, redundancy, and clean builds.

AETHRA redefines the conventional drone stack by consolidating flight control processing, power distribution and regulation, redundant inertial and environmental sensing, onboard telemetry, and peripheral expansion into a single, integrated flight controller architecture.

The system combines the MCU stack, PDB, dual IMUs, dual barometers, magnetometer, telemetry interfaces, and onboard diagnostics within a compact form factor, reducing interconnect complexity, improving EMI performance, and enabling deterministic, low-latency control behavior.

By reducing external wiring, connectors, and stacked boards, AETHRA significantly reduces EMI, vibration-induced faults, and assembly errors, while also simplifying the build process. The result is a flight controller that is not only more powerful, but also easier to integrate, more reliable in harsh environments, and scalable across racing, industrial, and research UAV platforms.
